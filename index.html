Welcome to my page! I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mathematics at Tufts University.

My current research interests are distance geometry, matrix completion, compressive sensing and structured dictionary learning. I am also interested in the application of these topics to structure prediction, signal processing and machine learning. In addition to these areas, I maintain a broad interest in applied mathematics and have also worked in problems in gas dynamics and shock waves.

My research is partially supported by National Science Foundation grant [NSF DMS 2304489].

I am looking for motivated students with a strong mathematical background. Please email me if you are interested in my research.
